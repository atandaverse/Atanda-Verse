Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How do online sessions work?

+

Our virtual sessions are conducted via Zoom or your preferred video platform. You'll receive a private link and detailed preparation guide 24 hours before your session. All you need is a quiet space and stable internet connection.

Are the sessions really free?

+

Yes! We're offering completely free sessions during our launch period to build our community and gather testimonials. No hidden fees, no credit card required. This is our gift to you.

What if I miss a scheduled session?

+

Life happens! Just give us 24 hours notice and we'll reschedule at no cost. We offer flexible timing to accommodate your schedule, including evening and weekend slots.

What should I expect from my first session?

+

Your first session focuses on understanding your current situation, identifying mental blocks, and creating a clear vision for your path forward. You'll leave with actionable insights and a personalized clarity plan.

Is this coaching, therapy, or counselling?

+

Clarity sessions are coaching-based, not therapy. We help you gain clarity, set direction, and take meaningful action. For clinical mental health needs, we recommend a licensed therapist.

Can I do sessions in Yoruba, Igbo, or Pidgin?

+

Absolutely. Some things are best expressed in the language you think and feel in. Sessions can be conducted in English, Pidgin, Yoruba, or Igbo — whatever helps you express yourself most authentically.

Do I need to prepare anything beforehand?

+

You'll receive a short guide 24 hours before your session with 3 reflection questions. All you need is a quiet space, stable internet, and an open mind. Come as you are.

What if the session doesn't help me?

+

During the free period there's zero financial risk. If you feel it didn't add value, tell us honestly — your feedback helps us improve. Our goal is genuine transformation, not just a session completed.
