What clarity work is

Clarity work is a forward-looking conversation and resource experience designed to help you name what is happening, organize your thoughts, identify next steps, and make more intentional decisions.

What it is not

  • It does not diagnose, treat, or manage any medical or mental health condition.
  • It does not replace licensed professional advice from a doctor, therapist, counsellor, lawyer, accountant, or financial adviser.

  • It does not guarantee personal, career, relationship, academic, financial, or business outcomes.

If you are in danger, considering self-harm, or experiencing a mental health crisis, contact local emergency services or a licensed mental health professional immediately. Do not rely on Atanda Verse content, forms, comments, email, or WhatsApp messages for crisis support.
