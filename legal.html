⚠️ Atanda Verse offers coaching-based clarity sessions, not therapy, counselling, or any form of clinical mental health treatment. Our services are not a substitute for professional psychological, psychiatric, or medical care.

What Clarity Coaching Is

Clarity coaching is a forward-focused, goal-oriented conversation designed to help you gain perspective, identify mental blocks, and create actionable plans. It draws on coaching methodologies and is designed for people who are mentally well and seeking personal growth, direction, or clarity.

What Clarity Coaching Is Not

  • It is not therapy, psychotherapy, or counselling
  • It does not diagnose, treat, or manage mental health conditions
  • It is not a crisis intervention service
  • It does not replace advice from licensed medical or mental health professionals

If You Need Clinical Support

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of self-harm, or symptoms of a clinical condition, please contact a licensed mental health professional or your nearest emergency service. In Nigeria, you can reach the Mental Health Foundation Nigeria or contact LASUTH Psychiatry department on +234 (0) 1 793 0000.
