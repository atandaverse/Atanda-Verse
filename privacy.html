PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 3, 2026. This explains what Atanda Verse collects, why it is used, where it may be processed, and how you can exercise your data rights.

Data Use Sharing Rights Storage Terms
What we collect

Personal Data We Handle

We collect only the information needed to run the website, respond to people, deliver sessions and resources, understand content performance, and protect the service.

Information you provide

  • Registration details: name, email address, WhatsApp or phone number, preferred session type, and optional goals or context.
  • Newsletter details: email address, name where provided, subscription source, and delivery tags.
  • Comments and testimonials: name, email, text, role, image, or story details you choose to submit.
  • Messages: email, WhatsApp, contact form, or support messages you send to us.
  • Vault or library access details: access requests, eligibility, saved access status, and resource activity where needed.

Information generated by site use

  • Basic analytics events such as viewed pages, blog post IDs, clicked links, referring page, session identifiers, timestamps, language, timezone, viewport size, and browser/user-agent details.
  • Admin and operational logs needed to diagnose errors, prevent abuse, and confirm whether emails, notifications, registrations, or content actions were processed.
  • Browser storage preferences such as theme selection, cached site settings, campaign display state, and admin-only credentials where an administrator logs in.
Why we use it

How We Use Data

  • To process registrations, schedule sessions, send confirmations, and contact you about the service you requested.
  • To deliver newsletters, welcome guides, resource links, and updates you opted into.
  • To moderate comments, publish approved testimonials, and protect the integrity of the blog and library.
  • To understand which articles, campaigns, pages, and resources are working so the site can improve.
  • To send administrator copies or notifications of registrations, subscriber actions, comments, and service events.
  • To investigate errors, prevent abuse, secure restricted areas, and meet legal or operational obligations.

Legal basis

Depending on the interaction, we may rely on your consent, the need to perform or prepare a service you requested, legitimate operational interests, legal compliance, or your explicit submission of information for publication.

Processors

Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share or process data through service providers that help run the site and deliver the service.

  • Supabase or similar database/storage infrastructure for forms, posts, comments, registrations, subscribers, analytics, and admin settings.
  • Vercel or similar hosting infrastructure for serving the website and routing requests.
  • Email and notification providers for welcome emails, admin copies, confirmations, and support replies.
  • WhatsApp, email clients, video meeting tools, payment providers, fonts, and other external services you choose to use or that are needed to deliver a requested service.

Where service providers process data outside Nigeria, your information may be transferred internationally subject to the provider's safeguards and applicable law.

Your control

Your Privacy Rights

Under Nigerian data protection principles, you may have rights to be informed, access your data, correct inaccurate data, object to certain processing, restrict processing, request portability, request erasure, withdraw consent where processing is consent-based, and complain to the relevant supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact support@atanda.site. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

Marketing messages

You may unsubscribe from marketing or newsletter communication. Service, registration, payment, security, and admin-related communication may still be sent where needed.

Children and young users

The site is not intended to collect data from children without appropriate guardian involvement. Users below 18 should not register independently.

Retention

Storage, Security & Cookies

Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably needed for the reason it was collected, including service delivery, support, accounting, security, analytics, dispute handling, and legal obligations. You can request deletion, subject to lawful retention needs.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no online service is perfectly secure. Avoid sending sensitive medical, legal, financial, or crisis information through public forms or comments.

Cookies and browser storage

The site may use localStorage, cookies, or similar browser storage for theme preferences, cached public settings, campaign display behavior, session identifiers, and admin-only access. You can clear browser storage through your browser settings, but some features may reset.

Contact

For privacy requests, email support@atanda.site. For general contact, email hello@atanda.site.
